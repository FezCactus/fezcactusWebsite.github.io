Javier L. Toquica

Artist, Animator and Coder

Born in the 80s, raised in the 90s... I came to LA but a few years ago to expand my knowledge and hopefully get a career in animation. Growing up I was raised on Nintendo and Cartoons. I've always held art in high regards and slowly learned through TV and books. During High School I was exposed to 3D animation, and learned about game design.

I attended the now defunct Art Institute of Ft Lauderdale to eventually gain a Bachelor's of Science in Video Game Art and Design. Afterwards I expanded that to learn 3D Character Animation. Lately I've been not only brushing up on my art but I have joined CS50 in hopes of expanding my skills further... to make my dream of making a game a reality!
